Company Overview

A Century of Excellence in the Pipe Industry

SOOR NEOGI COOMAR & CO PRIVATE LIMITED (SNC) is a Human Quality Development and Customer Centric Organization where Quality, Reliability and Customer Satisfaction is not just a business process but a reflection of every employee.

Started in the year 1908, we have been serving the Pipe Industry for more than a century. Even though it had been an old traditional business, SNC has taken up the challenge of transforming itself into a modern organization guided by globalization and modern business strategies.
